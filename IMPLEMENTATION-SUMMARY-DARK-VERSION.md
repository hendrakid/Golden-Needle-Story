## 📝 WRITING GUIDELINES

### When Writing Bungsu (Spirit):
- ✅ Keep her CHEERFUL despite death
- ✅ Use childlike vocabulary but occasionally profound
- ✅ Show she misses physical touch but accepts it
- ✅ Include moments where she forgets she's dead (tries to touch, giggles)
- ✅ Make her Sulung's moral anchor (prevents him going too dark)
- ❌ DON'T make her vengeful or bitter
- ❌ DON'T have her want revenge (justice yes, but not personal vendetta)
- ❌ DON'T forget she's ALWAYS present (mention her in scenes)

### When Writing Sulung (with Jiwa Peri):
- ✅ Occasionally glances at "empty space" where Bungsu floats
- ✅ Responds to her dialogue (NPCs see him "talking to himself")
- ✅ Feels warmth when she tries to "hug" him
- ✅ Uses her guidance in decisions ("Bungsu would want me to...")
- ✅ Experiences guilt (using her soul to fly) but she reassures him
- ❌ DON'T forget to show others' confusion (they don't see her)
- ❌ DON'T make him seem crazy (he's aware she's real)

### Tone Consistency:
- **Bittersweet** is the core emotion (happy she's "here" but sad she's dead)
- **Tragic** but not nihilistic (love endures despite death)
- **Hopeful** in darkness (Bungsu's presence keeps Sulung from despair)
