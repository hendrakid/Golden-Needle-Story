## 🎬 FINAL VERIFICATION: DOES IT WORK?

### Test Question: "How does Sulung find Pulau Langit?"

**Answer (Following Quest Chain)**:

> Sulung starts with zero information. While registering as a hunter (Q004), he discovers old archives mentioning "Sky Territory" but no details. During the Hunter Exam (Q005), he overhears senior hunters discussing prohibited zones and smuggler contacts.
>
> While hunting a bounty in the eastern mountains (Q006), he coincidentally meets Taji, who shares stories of seeing "floating shadows at dawn" in those very mountains. Following up on the smuggler lead from Q005, Sulung contacts Licik (Q007), who provides a partial sky route map pointing to ancient ruins.
>
> Exploring those ruins (Q008), Sulung discovers map fragments with ancient symbols showing Sky Island's approximate location. Coincidentally, while rescuing Senja from a magical crisis (Q009), he learns she can decode ancient languages. Senja translates the map, revealing approach vectors and the name "Mount Awan Tinggi."
>
> Scholar Malik (Q010) confirms the location and provides the Sky Key blueprint needed to complete the ascent. The Neutral Council (Q011) provides exact coordinates in a private meeting. Infiltrating an eagle outpost (Q012), Sulung confirms Bungsu is alive and located in Tower Block D, Level 3.
>
> With all components gathered, Sulung forges the Sky Key (Q014) and uses the coordinates, tactical intel, and flight ability to assault Pulau Langit (Q016).
