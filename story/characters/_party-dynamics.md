## 🔗 Relationship Matrix

### Sulung's Relationships

| With | Initial Dynamic | Mid-Journey | Late-Game |
|------|----------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Taji** | Employer/employee professional distance | Mutual respect; Taji's pack instinct awakening | Taji fully protective; Sulung trusts Taji completely |
| **Senja** | Senja views as "interesting case study" | Senja inspired by Sulung's courage | Senja would die for Sulung; sees them as true hero |
| **Licik** | Licik skeptical of quest success | Licik secretly admires idealism | Licik loyal to death; "my captain" mentality |

### Inter-Ally Relationships

**Taji ↔ Senja**
- **Initial:** Taji dismissive ("bookworm"), Senja frustrated ("anti-intellectual")
- **Conflict Point:** Senja insists on research/planning; Taji wants action
- **Growth:** Taji teaches practical wisdom; Senja teaches strategy value
- **Late-Game:** Mutual respect; Taji becomes Senja's emotional anchor; Senja becomes Taji's tactical advisor
- **Key Scene:** Taji comforts Senja after Q017 (Bungsu's death) - "Grief isn't weakness, Books. It's proof you cared."

**Taji ↔ Licik**
- **Initial:** Immediate understanding (fellow outcasts)
- **Bond:** Closest friendship in party; trust is instant and mutual
- **Dynamic:** Taji = emotional support, Licik = tactical partner
- **Late-Game:** Would die for each other without hesitation; "pack" bond strongest here
- **Key Scene:** Licik opens up about dead sibling only to Taji; Taji shares exile story in return

**Senja ↔ Licik**
- **Initial:** MAXIMUM TENSION - privilege vs. poverty clash
- **Conflict:** Senja judges Licik's criminal past; Licik mocks Senja's sheltered life
- **Turning Point:** Licik reveals can't read; Senja teaches them (bonding through education)
- **Growth:** Senja learns about systemic injustice; Licik learns knowledge has value
- **Late-Game:** Unlikely best friends; fierce mutual protection; constant banter
- **Key Scenes:** 
  - Licik: "You had EVERYTHING and still complain!" Senja: "You think grief only counts if you're poor?!"
  - Later: Licik reading their first book (Senja's gift); Senja crying with pride

## 🎬 Scene-Writing Quick Reference

### Voice Cheat Sheet

**Sulung (Chicken):**
- Simple, heartfelt, emotional
- Hesitates when afraid; determined when thinking of Bungsu
- "Aku harus pergi. Bungsu menungguku."

**Taji (Ajag):**
- Terse, blunt, practical
- Hunting metaphors; drops sentence subjects
- "Three exits. Two guards. North wall—cleanest shot."

**Senja (Musang):**
- Formal, educated, rambling when excited
- "Actually..." / "Technically speaking..."
- "According to the Principles of Shadowcraft, we should—"

**Licik (Rubah):**
- Street-smart, sarcastic, deflects with humor
- Uses nicknames (Feathers, Longshot, Books)
- "Easy. I'll be back before you finish that spell, Books."

### Body Language Quick Reference

| Character | Happy | Angry | Afraid | Focused |
|-----------|-------|-------|--------|---------|
| **Sulung** | Feathers puff, tail up | Feathers bristle, wings spread | Feathers droop, trembling | Beak set, wings tight |
| **Taji** | Tail relaxed curve | Ears back, growl | Tail tucked, frozen | Ears forward, still |
| **Senja** | Tail curled happy, whiskers forward | Tail lashing, fur bristled | Whiskers flat, eyes wide | Tail curled tight, eyes glow |
| **Licik** | Rare tail wag | Ears flat, snarl | Tail tucked, flight response | Ears swivel, dead eyes |

## 📊 Relationship Evolution Timeline

### ACT 2A — Formation (Q006-Q010)

**After Q006 (Taji Recruited):**
- Sulung + Taji: Professional courtesy; Taji calls Sulung "the client"
- Dynamic: Leader + hired gun; minimal personal connection

**After Q009 (Senja Recruited):**
- Sulung + Senja: Curiosity; Senja sees quest as field research
- Taji + Senja: Immediate friction; Taji thinks Senja is liability
- Dynamic: Awkward trio; formal and distant

**Q010 (Midpoint Twist - Truth Revealed):**
- **PARADIGM SHIFT:** Quest isn't curse, it's tyranny
- Party bonds over shared purpose (justice, not just rescue)
- First genuine "team" moment; still guarded but united
- Taji starts using "we"; Senja starts trusting instinct over books

### ACT 2B — Bonding (Q011-Q015)

**After Q013 (Licik Recruited - if taken):**
- Taji + Licik: Instant kinship (outcasts recognize each other)
- Senja + Licik: CONFLICT; class/privilege clash
- Sulung mediates conflicts; becoming true leader
- Dynamic: Functional team with internal tensions

**Q015 (Point of No Return):**
- Party must choose path together (Justice/Mercy/Vengeance)
- First time all voices matter equally
- Conflicts surface but resolve through compromise
- Taji admits party is "pack"; Senja trusts party over books; Licik commits fully
- Dynamic: Chosen family forming

### ACT 3 — Tested Unity (Q016-Q020)

**Q017 (Bungsu's Death - CRITICAL MOMENT):**
- **Sulung:** Devastated; nearly breaks
- **Taji:** Protective mode; refuses to let Sulung fall; "I won't lose this pack"
- **Senja:** Guilt spiral; "I should've had a spell"; Taji pulls them out
- **Licik:** Shutdown; flashback to sibling's death; nearly flees; party stops them
- **Aftermath:** Party at lowest point but TOGETHER; mutual support prevents individual collapse
- **Result:** Bonds forged in shared grief; family is cemented

**Q018 (Jiwa Peri Revelation):**
- Party accepts spirit Bungsu as member
- Taji protective of "the kid"; Senja fascinated; Licik comforted (proof loss isn't absolute)
- Dynamic: Family protecting family

**Q020 (Final Battle):**
- All fight for each other, not just Sulung's quest
- Willingness to die for party; chosen family > blood
- Ending choice reflects collective values learned through journey

## ⚔️ Combat Synergy

### Combo Attacks (Party Cooperation)

**Taji + Senja: "Twilight Arrow"**
- Senja casts darkness zone
- Taji fires arrows through shadows (guided by Senja's magic)
- Enemies can't see incoming shots

**Taji + Licik: "Crossfire"**
- Taji calls targets; Licik flanks
- Pincer attack; enemies must choose which threat to face
- High coordination; works only after Q010 (trust built)

**Senja + Licik: "Shadow Strike"**
- Senja casts invisibility on Licik
- Licik assassinates high-value target undetected
- Requires perfect timing; late-game unlock

**Three-Way: "Pack Tactics" (Taji, Senja, Licik)**
- Taji marks targets (hunter's mark)
- Senja buffs party speed
- Licik and Taji coordinate rapid takedowns
- Sulung holds frontline (tank role)

**Full Party: "Desperate Stand"**
- Triggered when Sulung below 25% HP
- All allies prioritize Sulung's protection
- Defensive formation; no one breaks rank
- Symbolizes: "We don't abandon family"

### Indicators of Party Bonding

| Act | Taji | Senja | Licik |
|-----|------|-------|-------|
| **Early 2A** | "The client" | "Fascinating subject" | "Easy money" |
| **Late 2A** | "Sulung needs..." | "We should plan..." | "Feathers is growing on me" |

### Sleep Positions (Trust Indicator)

**Early Journey:**
- Taji: Away from fire, back to tree (alert, isolated)
- Senja: Tent, door open, staff in hand (cautious)
- Licik: Hidden spot, one eye open (paranoid)

**Mid Journey:**
- Taji: Near fire, still alert (pack perimeter)
- Senja: Tent, door closed (trusting)
- Licik: Visible spot, weapons nearby (conditional trust)

**Late Journey:**
- Taji: By fire, sleeping soundly (trusts pack to watch)
- Senja: No tent, sleeping near allies (full trust)
- Licik: Vulnerable positions, weapons away (family)

## 🎭 Conflict Points & Resolutions

### Major Party Conflicts

**Conflict 1: Senja vs. Licik (Early ACT 2B)**
- **Trigger:** Licik suggests robbing corrupt official; Senja says "theft is wrong regardless"
- **Licik:** "Easy for someone who never starved to say"
- **Senja:** "I lost my master! Don't lecture ME about suffering!"
- **Resolution:** Taji mediates; "You both lost people. Different doesn't mean less. Focus on mission."
- **Growth:** Both learn to see other's perspective; apology later (Senja: "I was privileged. I'm sorry." Licik: "You still hurt. I'm sorry too.")

**Conflict 2: Taji vs. Party (Mid ACT 2B)**
- **Trigger:** Dangerous situation; Taji wants to handle alone (old habit)
- **Party:** "We're COMING with you!"
- **Taji:** "I work alone! You'll just—"
- **Sulung:** "Get hurt? Die? That's what you think of us?"
- **Resolution:** Taji breaks down; admits fear of loss; "I can't lose another pack"
- **Growth:** Party reassures; "You're not responsible for everyone. We're responsible for EACH OTHER."

**Conflict 3: Licik's Past Returns (Late ACT 2B)**
- **Trigger:** Former bandit crew confronts party; wants Licik back
- **Crew:** "You betrayed us. Come back or we kill your new friends."
- **Licik:** (prepares to leave to protect party)
- **Party:** "We fight together. You're not going anywhere."
- **Resolution:** Party defeats crew together; Licik realizes "they chose me over safety"
- **Growth:** Licik stops running; accepts found family

## 📝 Writer's Checklist for Multi-Character Scenes

When writing scenes with multiple party members:

✅ **Voice Test:** Can you identify speaker without name tag?
✅ **Distinct Reactions:** Each character responds differently to same event
✅ **Relationship Dynamics:** Show current relationship stage (early distant → late bonded)
✅ **Body Language:** Use species-specific tells (ears, tails, etc.)
✅ **Role Consistency:** Tank/Archer/Mage/Assassin each contribute uniquely
✅ **Growth Indicators:** Show progression from earlier scenes
✅ **Conflict Balance:** Tension is realistic but doesn't break party
✅ **Emotional Beats:** Someone always notices when another is struggling

## 🔮 Post-Q017 Party Dynamics (Bungsu's Death)

**Immediate Aftermath:**
- **Sulung:** Catatonic; party must physically move them
- **Taji:** Goes into protector overdrive; snaps at anyone who gets too close to Sulung
- **Senja:** Guilt spiral; tries to research "spirit resurrection" frantically; Taji stops them
- **Licik:** Emotional shutdown; tries to leave; party physically restrains them

**Camp Scene (Night After):**
```
[Campfire. Silence. Everyone processing.]

Senja: "I should've... there had to be a spell, a counter, SOMETHING..."

Taji: [quiet] "There wasn't. We were outmatched. Accepting that isn't defeat."

Licik: [bitter laugh] "So what. We just... accept it? Move on?"

Sulung: [hollow voice] "We don't move on. We move FORWARD. Bungsu wouldn't want us to stop."

[Pause]

Taji: "...Pack stays together. No matter what. That's the rule."

Senja: "I'm not leaving. Even if... even if I'm useless."

Licik: [sighs] "Guess I'm not running either. Someone's gotta watch your backs."

Sulung: [first hint of emotion] "Thank you. All of you."

[First group hug. Awkward. Necessary. Healing begins.]
```

**Going Forward:**
- Party is CLOSER after shared trauma
- Protectiveness intensifies (everyone guards everyone)
- Spirit Bungsu becomes honorary fifth member
- Final battle motivation: "For Bungsu. For each other."
