## KEPALA DESA

**Role:** Mentor / Village Elder
**Species:** Goat (Kambing / Elder Goat)
**Age:** Elderly (60s-70s equivalent for goats in Rimbara)
**Archetype:** The Wise Elder / The Retired Warrior Mentor

### Core Identity
- **Want:** To guide and protect the village; to help Sulung grow strong enough for the journey ahead; to see the young generation break the cycle of fear imposed by Ancient Chicken-Eagle conflict
- **Need:** To balance wisdom with allowing young people to take risks and grow; to accept that he can't fight every battle for his people anymore (age and retirement)
- **Fear:** Losing more villagers to Rajawali or other threats; seeing Sulung fail and perish like warriors he's lost before; village falling into despair without strong leadership
- **Wound:** **Past failure as warrior** — unable to save comrades during a battle (possibly related to how he met/saved Tuan Larek); carries guilt from those he couldn't protect; this drives his protective nature toward Sulung
- **Lie They Believe:** "I must personally solve everything for my people" / "If I don't intervene, they will fail"
- **Truth They Must Learn:** "True leadership means empowering others to solve their own problems" / "Letting go is also a form of strength"

### Voice & Speech Pattern
- **Vocabulary:** Simple but wise; uses calm, measured words
- **Sentence Length:** Medium, deliberate; speaks in complete thoughts
- **Speech Quirks:** Comforting tone; offers guidance rather than commands
- **Under Pressure:** Remains calm and centered; voice like anchor in storm
- **Sample Lines:**
  - Normal: "Tenang, Nak. Kita akan cari Bungsu bersama."
  - Advice: "Takut itu wajar. Yang penting adalah mau melangkah meski takut. Jangan gegabah."
  - Teaching: "Ini bukan pedang sungguhan. Tapi tangan yang berani bisa menjadikannya berguna."
  - Encouraging: "Jika kau berhasil, aku akan menulis surat rekomendasi untuk pelatih pedang di Kota Anginbiru."

### Physical Description

**Species Traits (Goat/Kambing):**
- Covered in gray-white fur (age has whitened much of his coat)
- Short curved horns (dulled with age, but still present — sign of past warrior days)
- Rectangular pupils (goat characteristic — gives him wise, knowing gaze)
- Small beard tuft under chin (gray, well-groomed)
- Bipedal anthropomorphic stance; walks upright with slight stoop (age)
- Hoofed feet (adapted for upright walking but retains caprine features)

**Build:**
- Sturdy herbivore build, but aged — no longer in warrior prime
- Broad shoulders suggest past strength, now softened by years of peace
- Slight paunch (comfortable village life after retirement)
- Moves deliberately, with calm measured steps (contrast to nervous chickens)
- Height: Taller than chickens (Sulung comes up to his chest), but not towering

**Clothing/Equipment:**
- Berkain sederhana (simple traditional wrap/sarong)
- Earth-toned clothing that reflects village elder status without ostentation
- May wear symbol of office (carved wooden pendant, simple staff)
- No armor or weapons visible (retired warrior), but keeps wooden training sword for teaching

**Distinctive Features:**
- Gray-white fur that speaks of age and wisdom
- Eyes: deep, calm, knowing — have seen much
- Voice: deeper than chickens, naturally authoritative but gentle
- Gestures are gentle despite size: menepuk bahu (shoulder pat), menaruh tangan di kepala (hand on head)
- Presence is calming, not physically imposing — aura of stability
- Slight battle scars hidden under fur (hints of warrior past)

### Backstory
- **Current Role:** Leader (Kepala Desa) of Desa Lereng-Gunung, a mixed-species village of predominantly chickens and small prey animals
- **Warrior Past:** Former soldier/warrior in a conflict (war against monsters? Territory disputes? TBD exact conflict). Fought alongside Tuan Larek (Rottweiler) in past.
- **Life Debt Incident:** During a battle, Tuan Larek was gravely wounded and surrounded by enemies. Kepala Desa (then a younger warrior goat) risked his life to extract Tuan Larek from certain death. Tuan Larek has owed him a life debt ever since.
- **Retirement:** After the conflict, Kepala Desa retired to Desa Lereng-Gunung to lead and protect a peaceful community. Chose village elder role over continued fighting.
- **Connections:** Has maintained contact with Tuan Larek, who became a renowned combat instructor in Kota Anginbiru. Recommendation letters from Kepala Desa carry weight.
- **Knowledge of Legend:** As village elder, knows the Ancient Chicken-Eagle Legend intimately. Has witnessed previous kidnappings (this is not the first pink-feathered chicken taken). Feels helpless against ancient cycle.
- **Loss:** May have lost villagers or comrades to various threats over the years — this drives his protective nature and fear of losing Sulung.

### Teaching Philosophy
- "Mulailah dari hal kecil" — start small (slime quest before big journey)
- Believes in testing through action, not just words
- Provides tools (pedang kayu) but doesn't fight battles for others
- Balances urgency with preparation: allows time for training despite crisis

### Notes for Writers
- Speech should always feel grounded and reassuring — voice of calm authority
- Never patronizing — treats Sulung (chicken) with respect despite species size difference and inexperience
- **Cross-species mentorship:** Goat mentoring chicken should feel natural (Rimbara has no speciesism). Size difference used for comforting physical gestures, not dominance.
- Gives tasks that build confidence progressively (slime quest before eagle confrontation)
- **Physical gestures show care:** menepuk bahu (shoulder pat — his hand on Sulung's smaller shoulder), menaruh tangan di kepala (hand on head — fatherly affection)
- **Body language (goat elder):**
  - Calm: stands still, rectangular pupils focused, ears relaxed
  - Concerned: slight head tilt, ears forward, soft bleat-like sigh
  - Teaching: uses horns to gesture occasionally (point direction), hooves planted firmly (stance of authority)
- Knows when to explain and when to let experience teach
- Has faith in Sulung even when Sulung doubts self — sees potential beyond species limitations
- **Knows the legend well:** May privately doubt parts of it, but doesn't openly challenge (would cause village panic). Hints at complexity through careful word choice.
- When writing dialogue: calm, measured, uses simple but profound wisdom ("Takut itu wajar" / "Mulailah dari hal kecil")
