# Q005 — HUNTER EXAM: HUTAN KELAM TRIAL

**Type:** Main Quest (ACT 1 FINALE)
**Act:** ACT 1
**Giver:** Hunter Association Proctor
**Location:** Hunter Association HQ (Kota Anginbiru) → Hutan Kelam (Dark Forest)
**Estimated Playtime:** Long (30-40 minutes including boss fight)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> After completing basic combat training under Tuan Larek and proving capability through dummy/sparring quests, Sulung is ready for the Hunter Association Exam. This official test will grant Hunter License — key to accessing dangerous zones, accepting high-rank quests, and eventually reaching territories near Pulau Langit. But the exam is no formality. Candidates face REAL danger in Hutan Kelam, a monster-infested forest where many have failed... and some never returned.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Determination + Nervousness (this is the real test)

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Pass Hunter Exam by completing trial objectives in Hutan Kelam; earn Hunter License
**Real Story:** First REAL test of Sulung's growth from helpless witness (Scene 01) to capable fighter; ACT 1 graduation moment before journey truly begins
**Thematic Question:** Has Sulung earned the right to pursue Rajawali? Is training enough to face real danger?

---

### 🗺️ Quest Structure

#### Phase 1 — REGISTRATION & BRIEFING
**Objective:** Register for Hunter Exam at Association HQ
**Location:** Hunter Association Building (Kota Anginbiru)
**Narrative Beat:** Sulung meets other exam candidates (mixed species); receives exam briefing from stern Proctor; learns objectives and dangers
**Player Discovery:** Not all candidates are heroes — some seek glory, money, or violence (foreshadows later moral choices)
**Emotional Target:** Anticipation + First taste of larger world beyond village

**Exam Briefing:**
```
Proctor (grizzled Bear): "Listen up, rookies. Hunter Exam ain't a game. You'll enter Hutan Kelam 
in groups of 3-5. Objectives are simple:

1. Hunt 5 Forest Slimes (proof of basic combat)
2. Collect 3 Moonflower Herbs (proof of navigation & survival skills)
3. Return alive within 2 hours (proof of time management)

OPTIONAL BONUS: Defeat the Bandit Leader rumored to operate in western quadrant. High reward, 
high risk. Most of you won't even find him. Those who do... well, we'll see.

Failure conditions: Die, abandon teammates, or exceed time limit. Questions?"

[Candidates murmur nervously]

Proctor: "Good. Form your teams. Exam starts in 10 minutes."
```

**🔍 [NEW] Eavesdropping Opportunity — Intel Gathering:**

While waiting for exam to begin, Sulung overhears senior hunters talking near equipment lockers:

```
[Senior Hunter 1 (Leopard) and Senior Hunter 2 (Hawk) discussing past missions]

Leopard: "...heard they're expanding prohibited zones AGAIN. Eastern mountains now off-limits."

Hawk: "Eagle patrols. They don't want anyone near their routes."

Leopard: "Routes to what? What're they hiding up there?"

Hawk: [lowers voice] "You really want to know? Friend of mine— works the black market— 
says there's smuggler network that moves goods PAST eagle checkpoints. Sky route maps. 
Prohibited coordinates."

Leopard: "Illegal as hell."

Hawk: "Sure. But if you needed to reach somewhere eagles don't want you going... 
only option. Guy goes by 'Licik the Fox.' Operates out of port district."

Leopard: "You trust smugglers?"

Hawk: [shrugs] "I trust money. And Licik charges enough to BE trustworthy. Just saying— 
if someone ever needed to reach, say, Sky Island..."

Leopard: [laughs nervously] "Who'd be crazy enough to try THAT?"

[Sulung, listening from nearby, makes mental note]

Sulung (Internal): "Licik. Port district. Sky route maps. Remember that."
```

**Intel Gained:**
- **Smuggler Contact:** Licik the Fox (port district) — has sky route maps
- **Eastern Mountains:** Prohibited zone (eagles don't want people there) → becomes relevant in Q006-Q008
- **Confirmation:** Reaching eagle territories requires illegal/alternative methods
- **Sky Island mentioned:** First time Sulung hears the NAME of the place he's looking for

**Story Impact:** This eavesdropping scene plants the seeds for:
- Q007 (Smuggler Deal) — Sulung now knows WHO to contact
- Q006-Q008 (Eastern Mountains exploration) — knows it's a restricted area worth investigating

**Team Formation Choice:**
- **Solo:** Sulung goes alone (harder but full XP/loot)
- **Join Team:** NPC candidates invite Sulung (easier, share XP, introduces potential allies/rivals)

---

#### Phase 2 — HUTAN KELAM TRIAL
**Trigger:** Enter Dark Forest with team (or solo)
**New Objective:** Complete 3 exam tasks within time limit
**The Twist/Reveal:** Forest is MORE dangerous than briefed — higher-level monsters, environmental hazards, bandit ambushes
**Player Choice:** Prioritize speed (rush objectives) OR thorough exploration (find secrets, help other teams)
**Emotional Target:** Tension + Growing confidence (Sulung realizes he CAN fight now)

**Gameplay — Forest Exploration:**
- **Objective 1:** Hunt 5 Forest Slimes
  - Basic monsters, good for applying Tuan Larek's training
  - Teaches dodge-timing, combo execution
  - Callback to Q002 (dummy training) — but these slimes fight back!
  
- **Objective 2:** Collect 3 Moonflower Herbs
  - Scattered across map, requires exploration
  - Some guarded by monsters or in dangerous terrain
  - Tests navigation, awareness
  
- **Objective 3:** Return Alive
  - Time limit adds pressure (2 hours = ~15-20 minutes real-time)
  - Encounters with stronger monsters if straying from path
  - Optional: Help struggling teammates vs leave them (moral choice)

**Random Encounter (Mid-Quest):**
```
[Sulung hears screams — another exam team under attack by pack of wolves]

Struggling Candidate (Rabbit): "Help! Someone—!"

[Player Choice:]
1. HELP THEM (spend time, risk time limit, gain gratitude)
2. IGNORE (stay on mission, cold but pragmatic)
3. LOOT CORPSES if they die (darkest choice, fails "honor" check)

[Choice affects reputation and potential ally recruitment later]
```

---

#### Phase 3 — OPTIONAL BOSS: BANDIT LEADER ⚔️
**Final Objective:** Track and defeat Bandit Leader (OPTIONAL)
**Stakes:** Bonus reward + Hunter Association rep + mini-boss experience OR risk failure/death
**Major Choice:** Pursue bandit OR return safely with minimum requirements completed
**Emotional Target:** Risk vs Reward — "Am I strong enough for this?"

**Tracking the Bandit:**
- Clues scattered in forest (footprints, camp remnants, intimidated NPCs)
- Leads to western quadrant clearing
- **BANDIT LEADER** waiting (knew exam was today, set ambush for rookies)

**MINI-BOSS FIGHT — Bandit Leader**

**Enemy Profile:**
- **Species:** Wolf OR Fox (predator, cunning)
- **Name:** "Scarface" OR "Krait the Red"
- **Personality:** Ruthless, mocking, enjoys preying on weak
- **Motivation:** Robs/kills exam candidates for gear and sport

**Boss Mechanics (Phase 1):**
- Fast melee attacks (quicker than previous enemies)
- Dodge-heavy patterns (teaches player to be patient)
- Taunt dialogue mid-fight ("You chickens break so easily!")
- **Weakness:** Overconfident, leaves openings after big attacks

**Mid-Fight Dialogue:**
```
Bandit Leader: "Another chicken trying to play hero? Where's your wings, bird?"

Sulung: [parries attack] "I don't need wings to beat you!"

Bandit Leader: [laughs] "Brave words. Let's see if—"

[Sulung lands solid hit, Bandit staggers]

Bandit Leader: [wipes blood, snarls] "Lucky shot. I'll make you regret that."
```

**Phase 2 Trigger:** Reduce boss to 50% HP
- Bandit calls reinforcements (2-3 bandit goons appear)
- Player must decide: Focus boss OR clear adds first
- Teaches target prioritization

**Victory Condition:** Defeat Bandit Leader
- **Kill Option:** Execute defeated bandit (dark choice, extra loot)
- **Spare Option:** Tie up and report to authorities (lawful, reputation boost)
- **Interrogate Option:** Ask about eagle sightings/Sky Island rumors (narrative clue)

---

#### Phase 4 — RETURN & GRADUATION
**Outcome:** Return to Hunter Association HQ with completed objectives (and optionally defeated bandit proof)
**World Change:** Sulung officially becomes Hunter; unlocks new quest tiers and travel permissions
**Character Change:** Transformed from village boy to recognized warrior; ACT 1 complete
**Echo Forward:** Hunter License necessary for ACT 2 journey into dangerous territories

**Graduation Scene:**
```
[Sulung returns to HQ, presents collected herbs + slime cores + (optional) Bandit Leader's weapon]

Proctor: [inspects evidence] "Hmm. Forest Slimes, check. Moonflowers, check."

[If Bandit Leader defeated:]
Proctor: [picks up bandit's weapon, surprised] "You took down Scarface? Damn. We've been 
trying to catch that bastard for months. You're either lucky or good."

Sulung: "Bit of both."

Proctor: [grudging respect] "Alright, chicken. You pass. Here."

[Hands over HUNTER LICENSE — official metal badge]

Proctor: "Don't make me regret this. And... good luck with whatever you're really after. 
Something tells me you're not in this for the money."

Sulung: [looks at license, thinks of Bungsu] "No. I'm not."
```

**If Solo:** Proctor notes Sulung completed exam alone (rare, impressive)
**If Helped Others:** Rescued candidates thank Sulung (potential future allies flagged)
**If Ignored/Looted:** Proctor heard reports, warns Sulung about ethics

## 💡 Design Notes

**Why This Quest Matters:**
1. **Skill Check:** Tests everything learned in Q001-Q004
2. **Graduation Moment:** Official transition from trainee to hunter
3. **First Real Danger:** Stakes are real (can actually fail/die)
4. **Optional Challenge:** Bandit boss teaches "not everything is mandatory but rewards exist"
5. **Moral Choices:** Help/ignore/loot introduces alignment system subtly

**Boss Difficulty:**
- Easier than ACT 2/3 bosses but harder than anything faced so far
- Designed to be beatable with ACT 1 skills but challenging
- Optional nature means player can skip if struggling

**Pacing:**
- Forest exploration breaks up combat monotony
- Time limit adds urgency without being oppressive
- Optional objectives let players control pace
