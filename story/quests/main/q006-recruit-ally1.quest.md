# Q006 — WINGS OF TRUST: RECRUITING ALLY 1

**Type:** Main Quest (ACT 2A — First Ally Recruitment)
**Act:** ACT 2A
**Giver:** Rumor/Notice Board in Hunter Association
**Location:** Mountain Pass → Cliff Village (Ally 1's hometown)
**Estimated Playtime:** Medium (20-30 minutes)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> After earning Hunter License, Sulung learns of mercenary hawk known for aerial reconnaissance skills. Rumor says this hawk helps travelers navigate dangerous mountain routes for fair price. But more importantly: they can FLY. If Sulung wants to reach Sky Island eventually, he needs someone who understands aerial navigation. First step: recruit Ally 1.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Hope (someone who can help) + Caution (can they be trusted?)

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Hire hawk mercenary as guide/companion for journey
**Real Story:** First trust-building with non-chicken ally; establishing party dynamics; learning not all flying species serve eagles
**Thematic Question:** Can predator and prey species work together despite ancient prejudices?

---

### 🗺️ Quest Structure

#### Phase 1 — FINDING THE HAWK
**Objective:** Travel to Cliff Village, locate Ally 1 (Hawk/Falcon)
**Narrative Beat:** Sulung meets Ally 1 mid-job (guiding merchant caravan); observes their competence
**Player Discovery:** Ally 1 is professional, not particularly friendly initially; views Sulung as potential client, nothing more
**Emotional Target:** Curiosity + Initial distrust (they're a bird of prey, after all)

**First Meeting:**
```
[Cliff Village — windy mountain settlement, precarious buildings on cliffside]

[Ally 1 lands gracefully, folding wings, speaking to merchant]

Ally 1 (Hawk): "That'll be 200 gold for safe passage through Wyrm's Pass. Pleasure doing business."

Merchant: "Worth every coin. Those bandits didn't stand a chance against your dive attacks."

[Ally 1 notices Sulung watching]

Ally 1: [sharp eyes assess Sulung] "You need something, chicken? Lost?"

Sulung: "I'm looking for an aerial guide. Heard you're the best."

Ally 1: [raises eyebrow] "Aerial guide? You DO realize chickens don't fly, right?"

Sulung: "Not yet. But I'm working on it. And I need someone who knows the skies."

Ally 1: [intrigued despite herself] "Interesting. What's the job?"
```

---

#### Phase 2 — THE TEST JOB
**Trigger:** Ally 1 offers trial contract: escort her on dangerous delivery job
**New Objective:** Prove competence and trustworthiness through action
**Player Choice:** Accept immediately OR negotiate terms
**Emotional Target:** Proving yourself to someone skeptical

**Trial Mission — Bandit Ambush:**
- Escort Ally 1 delivering package through mountain pass
- Bandits attack (aerial + ground)
- **Combat Test:** Sulung must hold his own while Ally 1 handles aerial threats
- **Trust Moment:** Ally 1 gets grounded/injured mid-fight; Sulung must protect her

**Critical Scene:**
```
[Mid-combat, Ally 1's wing clipped by arrow, crashes to ground]

Ally 1: [grimacing, trying to stand] "Damn it—!"

Bandit: [advancing with blade] "Not so tough without your wings, bird!"

[PLAYER CHOICE:]
→ PROTECT ALLY 1 (fight bandits to defend her)
→ PRIORITIZE MISSION (grab package, leave her)

IF PROTECT:
[Sulung steps between bandit and Ally 1]

Sulung: "You want her? Go through me first."

Ally 1: [surprised] "You... you're defending me?"

Sulung: [fighting off bandit] "We're in this together. Right?"

[After combat victory]

Ally 1: [standing, testing wing] "I... didn't expect that. Most clients would've run. 
Or worse, left me for dead."

Sulung: "I'm not like most clients."

Ally 1: [slight smile — first warmth] "No. You're not."
```

---

#### Phase 3 — THE CONVERSATION
**Final Objective:** After trial job, Ally 1 and Sulung talk; backstory revealed
**Narrative Beat:** Ally 1 opens up about why she became mercenary; family issues; distrust of authority
**Emotional Target:** Connection forming; mutual respect

**Backstory Reveal:**
```
[Campfire after successful delivery, Ally 1 bandaging wing]

Sulung: "Can I ask... why do you work alone? Most hawks I've heard of fly in formations."

Ally 1: [pause] "Family disagreement. My clan serves the Eagle Court. Scouts, messengers, 
enforcers. I... didn't want that life."

Sulung: [careful] "Eagle Court? As in... Rajawali's forces?"

Ally 1: [bitter laugh] "Not directly. But close enough. When I refused to swear fealty, 
they disowned me. Said I was 'disgrace to sky-kind.'"

Sulung: "So you became mercenary."

Ally 1: "Better than being someone's pawn. At least now, I choose my contracts. I choose 
who I help." [looks at Sulung] "Speaking of which... what's YOUR story? Why does a flightless 
chicken need aerial navigation?"

Sulung: [hesitates, then decides to trust] "My sister was taken. By an eagle. To Sky Island."

Ally 1: [expression shifts — understanding, sympathy] "...I see."

Sulung: "I know you have no reason to help me. We just met. And your people and mine... 
we're not exactly allies historically."

Ally 1: "Your people didn't disown you for wanting freedom. Mine did. If you're going 
against Eagle Court... maybe we're more alike than different."

[Extends talon/wing for handshake]

Ally 1: "I'm in. Not as mercenary. As... partner."

Sulung: [shakes hand/wing] "Thank you."

Ally 1: "Don't thank me yet. Sky Island is suicide. But... if anyone deserves help trying, 
it's someone fighting for family."
```

### 💬 Key Dialogue

**On Trust:**
> Ally 1: "Why would a chicken trust a hawk? We're predators."  
> Sulung: "And eagles are worse. Enemy of my enemy?"  
> Ally 1: "That's... pragmatic. I can work with pragmatic."

**On Family:**
> Ally 1: "You're risking your life for your sister. I walked away from mine. Who's the better sibling?"  
> Sulung: "The one who chooses their own path. You made yours. I'm making mine."
