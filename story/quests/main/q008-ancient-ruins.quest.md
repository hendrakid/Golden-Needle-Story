# Q008 — ECHOES OF THE PAST: ANCIENT RUINS

**Type:** Main Quest (ACT 2A — Puzzle/Exploration)
**Act:** ACT 2A
**Giver:** Map fragment from Q007 OR local rumors
**Location:** Forgotten Temple Ruins (Pre-Eagle Era)
**Estimated Playtime:** Long (30-40 minutes — exploration heavy)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> Following leads about eagle history, Sulung discovers ruins predating current Eagle-Chicken conflict. Ancient temple holds murals, carvings, and relics from time BEFORE fabricated legend. If Scholar Malik's theory (Q010) is correct, this place holds REAL history — before Rajawali rewrote it. Exploring ruins may provide evidence... or raise more questions.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Archaeological curiosity + Hope (truth might be here)

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Explore ruins for treasure/artifacts
**Real Story:** Discovery of visual evidence that challenges legend; foreshadows Q010 revelation; environmental storytelling about pre-conflict era
**Thematic Question:** What gets lost when history is rewritten by victors?

---

### 🗺️ Quest Structure

#### Phase 1 — ENTRY & INITIAL EXPLORATION
**Objective:** Navigate overgrown ruins, reach inner temple
**Narrative Beat:** Ruins are ANCIENT — older than any structure Sulung has seen; nature reclaimed them
**Player Discovery:** Architecture shows chickens and eagles coexisting peacefully in carvings
**Emotional Target:** Wonder + Confusion (why doesn't this match the legend?)

**Entrance Discovery:**
```
[Massive stone temple half-swallowed by jungle vines]

Ally 1: [circling above] "This place is OLD. Like... centuries before Eagle Court old."

Sulung: [examining weathered carvings at entrance] "Look at these reliefs..."

[Carvings show: Eagles and chickens SIDE BY SIDE — not in conflict]
[Scenes of trade, cooperation, shared festivals]

Sulung: [touching carving] "They're... working together? But the legend says we were always enemies."

Ally 1: [lands beside him] "Or the legend lied. Come on. Let's see what's inside."
```

---

#### Phase 2 — PUZZLE CHAMBERS (Environmental Storytelling)
**Trigger:** Enter temple interior; must solve puzzles to progress
**New Objective:** Navigate three themed chambers; each reveals historical truth fragment
**Gameplay:** Light puzzle mechanics (pressure plates, beam reflection, matching symbols)
**Emotional Target:** Discovery (truth revealed through environment, not dialogue)

**CHAMBER 1 — Hall of Unity:**
- **Visual:** Murals of mixed-species council (chickens, eagles, others) governing together
- **Puzzle:** Arrange statues to match mural (cooperation theme)
- **Discovery:** Inscription: "In unity, we soar. In division, we fall."
- **Implication:** No ancient chicken-eagle war — they were allies

**CHAMBER 2 — Vault of Trade:**
- **Visual:** Market scene — eagles provide aerial transport, chickens provide ground agriculture
- **Puzzle:** Balance scales with economic goods (symbiotic relationship)
- **Discovery:** Trade ledgers showing mutual benefit, NO conflict
- **Implication:** Economic partnership, not predator-prey dynamic

**CHAMBER 3 — Sanctuary of Peace:**
- **Visual:** Ceremonial hall where BOTH species worshipped together
- **Puzzle:** Light beams reveal hidden text when aligned
- **Discovery:** Peace treaty from 400 years ago — treaty ENDING a brief conflict through reconciliation
- **Implication:** Any ancient war was resolved centuries ago; current "legend" is recent fabrication

**Key Revelation Scene:**
```
[Chamber 3, light beams illuminate hidden inscription]

Sulung: [reading ancient text aloud] "Let this mark the end of strife. Eagle and chicken, 
bound no more by blood, but by brotherhood. May our children never know war between our kinds."

[Date carved: 412 years ago]

Ally 1: "If this treaty ended war four centuries ago... then what's the 'ancient grudge' 
Rajawali claims?"

Sulung: [realization dawning] "There isn't one. Or there wasn't. Not until someone decided 
to pretend there was."

Ally 1: "Someone rewrote history. Made peace look like eternal conflict."

Sulung: [anger building] "To justify... everything he's doing now."

[Environmental cue: Distant rumble, ruins shake slightly]

Ally 1: "We should document this and leave. Place feels unstable."
```

---

#### Phase 3 — THE GUARDIAN (Optional Mini-Boss)
**Final Objective:** Deep in ruins, ancient guardian golem awakens (defensive mechanism)
**Stakes:** Defeat guardian to access central vault OR flee with current discoveries
**Major Choice:** Fight for complete evidence OR preserve life/time
**Emotional Target:** Risk vs Reward (how much danger is truth worth?)

**Guardian Encounter:**
```
[Central vault, ancient construct activates — stone golem]

Guardian: [ancient mechanical voice] "Intruders... Sanctuary... Violated..."

Ally 1: "It's a defense system! Still active after centuries!"

Sulung: "We don't want to fight! We're seeking truth!"

Guardian: "Truth... sealed. Protected. Must... eliminate... trespassers."

[PLAYER CHOICE:]
→ FIGHT GUARDIAN (boss battle — reward: complete historical records)
→ FLEE RUINS (avoid combat — keep current discoveries, miss vault contents)
```

**IF FIGHT GUARDIAN (Boss Mechanics):**
- Stone construct, high defense, slow but powerful
- Weak points: Ancient runes on body (must target specifically)
- **Ally 1 Assistance:** Aerial strikes to distract while Sulung attacks weak points
- **Victory Reward:** Access to vault with COMPLETE pre-conflict records

**IF FLEE:**
- Keep evidence already found (murals, inscriptions)
- Ruins collapse partially behind Sulung (atmospheric escape sequence)
- Miss vault but avoid danger

---

#### Phase 4 — AFTERMATH & REFLECTION
**Outcome:** Sulung leaves ruins with physical evidence of peace era
**World Change:** Player has concrete proof legend is fabricated (sets up Q010)
**Character Change:** Sulung realizes fight is bigger than personal revenge
**Echo Forward:** Evidence can be shown to Scholar Malik (Q010) and used in final confrontation

**Exit Scene:**
```
[Outside ruins, Sulung and Ally 1 catch breath]

Ally 1: "So. Chickens and eagles were never eternal enemies. Someone made that up."

Sulung: [holding rubbing of peace treaty inscription] "And used the lie to justify... 
how many deaths? How many Bungsus over the years?"

Ally 1: "Hundreds. Maybe thousands. Across generations."

Sulung: [quiet fury] "I'm going to expose this. Not just rescue Bungsu. Expose everything."

Ally 1: "That's... ambitious. And dangerous. You ready for that fight?"

Sulung: "I have to be. For everyone who believed the lie. Who died because of it."

[Quest Complete — Evidence Obtained]
```
