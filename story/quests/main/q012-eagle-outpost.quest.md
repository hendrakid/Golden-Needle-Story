# Q012 — SHADOW STRIKE: EAGLE OUTPOST INFILTRATION

**Type:** Main Quest (ACT 2B — Stealth/Combat Hybrid)
**Act:** ACT 2B
**Giver:** Self-motivated (gather intelligence on Sky Island defenses)
**Location:** Eagle Military Outpost (border garrison)
**Estimated Playtime:** Long (30-40 minutes)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> Armed with EXACT COORDINATES from the Neutral Council (Q011), Sulung now knows where Sky Island floats: above Mount Awan Tinggi. But before attempting direct assault, tactical intelligence is critical. Eagle border outpost holds detailed patrol schedules, defense grid layouts, and — most importantly — PRISONER MANIFESTS confirming Bungsu's current status. Additionally, the outpost armory contains Eagle Talons (required for Sky Key forging in Q014). Infiltrate, gather intelligence, steal talon, escape.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Determination + Tactical Focus (final preparation before the rescue)

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Infiltrate eagle outpost, steal intelligence documents
**Real Story:** First direct confrontation with eagle military; testing skills before final assault; glimpse of Bungsu's status in captured records (alive but in danger)
**Thematic Question:** How far into enemy territory can determination take you before fear stops progress?

---

### 🗺️ Quest Structure

#### Phase 1 — PLANNING THE INFILTRATION
**Objective:** Scout outpost perimeter, choose infiltration method
**Narrative Beat:** Allies provide different expertise; player chooses approach
**Player Discovery:** Multiple entry points, each with risks/rewards
**Emotional Target:** Strategic thinking (this requires planning, not just courage)

**Planning Scene:**
```
[Overlooking eagle outpost from distant ridge — fortified garrison, guards visible]

Ally 1 (Hawk): [aerial view] "Three guard towers. Rotating aerial patrols every 15 minutes. 
Main gate heavily fortified."

Ally 2 (Stealth Expert): [examining blueprints] "But there's maintenance tunnel under 
east wall. Leads to basement archives. That's where records would be."

Sulung: "Three approaches then. Aerial, frontal, or underground."

Ally 1: "Aerial is fastest but riskiest. I can carry you, but if spotted mid-air... 
we're targets."

Ally 2: "Tunnel is my specialty. Quiet. Slow. But if we get trapped down there..."

Sulung: "And frontal?"

Ally 1: [laughs] "Suicide. Unless you want distraction while someone sneaks?"

[PLAYER CHOOSES APPROACH]
```

**Three Infiltration Routes:**

**ROUTE A — AERIAL DROP (WITH ALLY 1):**
- Ally 1 carries Sulung, drops on rooftop during patrol gap
- Fast entry but high detection risk
- **Advantage:** Quick access to command tower (intel stored there)
- **Disadvantage:** If spotted, aerial chase sequence (high intensity)

**ROUTE B — TUNNEL CRAWL (WITH ALLY 2):**
- Stealth through maintenance tunnels to basement
- Slow, methodical, puzzle elements (locked doors, tripwires)
- **Advantage:** Low detection risk, access to archives
- **Disadvantage:** Time-consuming, risk of cave-in or trap activation

**ROUTE C — DISTRACTION ASSAULT (SPLIT PARTY):**
- Allies create frontal distraction (Ally 1 aerial feint, Ally 2 sets fires)
- Sulung infiltrates during chaos (hybrid stealth-combat)
- **Advantage:** Guards distracted, multiple objectives possible
- **Disadvantage:** Allies at risk, high alarm level after

#### Phase 2 — INFILTRATION GAMEPLAY
**Trigger:** Execute chosen route; navigate to intelligence vault
**New Objective:** Avoid/defeat guards, reach central archives
**The Twist:** Encounter imprisoned chicken from previous kidnappings (optional rescue)
**Emotional Target:** Tension (stealth gameplay) + Horror (see other victims)

**Inside the Outpost:**
```
[Sulung navigates corridors/tunnels based on route chosen]

[Overhears eagle guards talking:]

Guard 1: "Another shipment to Sky Island tomorrow. Three more pink-feathered chickens."

Guard 2: "What does Lord Rajawali even DO with them all?"

Guard 1: "Don't ask questions above your rank. Just process the paperwork."

[Sulung's internal reaction: more victims, time is running out]

[Optional discovery: Holding cells with recent captives]

Prisoner (Chicken): [weak, behind bars] "Please... help..."

[PLAYER CHOICE:]
→ RESCUE NOW (raises alarm but saves prisoners)
→ RESCUE LATER (promise to return, focus on intel first)
→ IGNORE (coldest option, maintains stealth)
```

#### Phase 3 — INTELLIGENCE ACQUISITION
**Final Objective:** Access archive vault, steal Sky Island defense plans + prisoner records
**Stakes:** Must obtain documents before alarm raised OR escape before guards overwhelm
**Major Discovery:** Bungsu's name found in "Active Prisoner" manifest — CONFIRMED ALIVE (for now)
**Emotional Target:** Relief (she's alive!) + Urgency (must hurry)

**Archive Scene:**
```
[Archive room — filing cabinets, maps on walls]

[Senja, if present, picks vault lock using minor magic]

Senja: "We're in. Grab what you need. We have maybe 5 minutes before patrol notices door."

[Sulung searches files]

Sulung: [finds folder] "Sky Island Defense Grid... patrol schedules... aerial countermeasures..."

[Cross-references with Council coordinates from Q011]

Sulung: "These patrol routes... they match the Council's intel. 15-mile perimeter, rotating shifts."

[Finds prisoner manifest]

Sulung: [hands trembling as he scans list] "No... no... no... BUNGSU!"

[Name appears: "BUNGSU - Pink/Yellow - Acquired 47 days ago - Status: ALIVE - Location: Tower Cell Block D"]

Taji: "You found her?"

Sulung: [clutching document] "She's alive. She's... she's in Tower Block D. North tower, 
level 3." [looks at Taji] "The coordinates from Council said North courtyard entry. If we 
reach the courtyard, her cell is accessible."

Senja: "Then we know EXACTLY where to go when we reach Sky Island."

Taji: "Good. Now let's get out before we join her."

[OPTIONAL: Steal Eagle Talon from armory on way out]

Sulung: [notices display case] "Wait. Eagle Talon. Ceremonial blade made from talon."

Senja: "That's the component Malik mentioned! For the Sky Key!"

Sulung: [breaks case, takes talon blade] "One more piece secured."
```

**INTEL & ITEMS ACQUIRED:**
- ✅ **Defense Grid Map:** Sky Island patrol schedules and aerial countermeasures
- ✅ **Prisoner Manifest:** BUNGSU CONFIRMED ALIVE, Tower Block D, North Tower Level 3
- ✅ **Precise Cell Location:** Can plan direct rescue route during Q016 assault
- ✅ **Eagle Talon (Item):** Component #4 for Sky Key forging (Q014)
- ✅ **Patrol Gaps:** Best approach window identified (dawn, as ancient map suggested)

[ALARM SOUNDS — guards detected intrusion]

Ally 1: "Spoke too soon! MOVE!"
```
