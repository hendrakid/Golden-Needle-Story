# Q013 — WINGS DIVIDED: THE EAGLE DEFECTOR (OPTIONAL)

**Type:** Optional Main Quest (ACT 2B — Third Ally Recruitment)
**Act:** ACT 2B
**Giver:** Rumor/Encounter during Q012 OR random event
**Location:** Neutral Territory Safehouse
**Estimated Playtime:** Medium (20-30 minutes)

---

### ⚠️ **OPTIONAL QUEST NOTICE**

This quest is **OPTIONAL** but provides significant advantages:
- Ally 3 (Eagle Defector) offers aerial expertise (better than Ally 1)
- Insider knowledge of Sky Island layout
- Moral complexity (recruiting from enemy species)
- Affects Q015 dialogue (Mercy path perspective)
- Makes Q019-Q020 easier (flight support, intel)

**Can be skipped** if player prefers smaller party or distrusts eagles entirely.

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> During Q012 escape OR through underground contacts, Sulung hears of eagle deserter hiding in neutral territory. This eagle defected from Rajawali's forces, claims to have moral objections to kidnapping operations. Could be genuine ally... or trap. Meeting them is risky, but potential reward — insider knowledge of Sky Island — is enormous.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Suspicion + Cautious Hope (can ANY eagle be trusted?)

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Investigate eagle defector; decide if trustworthy ally
**Real Story:** Moral complexity of judging individual vs. species; "not all eagles serve Rajawali" theme; player's prejudice tested
**Thematic Question:** Can you trust someone from enemy species? Should crimes of the many condemn the individual?

---

### 🗺️ Quest Structure

#### Phase 1 — THE MEETING
**Objective:** Locate defector's safehouse; arrange meeting
**Narrative Beat:** Tense first contact; defector is paranoid (hunted by both sides)
**Player Discovery:** Ally 3 is genuinely traumatized by what they witnessed serving Rajawali
**Emotional Target:** Suspicion slowly giving way to empathy

**Initial Contact:**
```
[Abandoned warehouse, neutral zone]

[Ally 3 (Eagle) perched in shadows, hooded]

Ally 3: "You're the chicken asking questions about Sky Island. Brave or suicidal?"

Sulung: [hand on weapon] "Depends who's asking."

Ally 3: [removes hood — eagle features, scarred] "Someone who used to serve there. 
Until I couldn't stomach it anymore."

[Allies react — Ally 1 tenses, Ally 2 ready to flee]

Ally 1 (Hawk): [hostile] "An EAGLE? Sulung, this is trap!"

Ally 3: [raises talons peacefully] "If I wanted you dead, I'd have alerted patrols. 
I didn't. I want... out. And maybe... to undo some of what I helped do."

Sulung: [studying Ally 3] "What did you do?"

Ally 3: [pained] "I guarded the cages. I watched them take children. I... followed orders. 
Until I couldn't."
```

---

#### Phase 2 — THE TEST (Proving Sincerity)
**Trigger:** Sulung demands proof of genuine defection
**New Objective:** Ally 3 must demonstrate trustworthiness through action
**Player Choice:** What test to impose (mercy, sacrifice, combat, information)
**Emotional Target:** Judgment (is confession enough, or must they earn redemption?)

**Test Options:**

**TEST A — INFORMATION PROOF:**
```
Sulung: "If you're genuine, give me something useful. Sky Island layout. Guard rotations."

Ally 3: [pulls out hand-drawn map] "I mapped everything during my final weeks there. 
Exits. Blind spots. Where they keep prisoners."

[Shows detailed map — confirms Bungsu's location matches Q012 intel]

Ally 3: "Tower Block D. That's where new captives go. Your sister... if she's there, 
this is her cell."

[Map accuracy can be verified — builds trust]
```

**TEST B — SACRIFICE PROOF:**
```
[Eagle patrol approaches safehouse during meeting]

Eagle Patrol Leader: [calling out] "Defector! We know you're in there! Surrender!"

Ally 3: [to Sulung] "If I go with them, they'll torture me. Execute me. But if I fight... 
I burn last bridge to my people."

[PLAYER CHOICE:]
→ DEMAND ALLY 3 FIGHT (prove allegiance through combat)
→ PROTECT ALLY 3 (fight patrol together, earn loyalty)
→ ABANDON ALLY 3 (let eagles take them, walk away)

IF PROTECT:
[Sulung and allies defend Ally 3]

Ally 3: [after battle, emotional] "You... you defended me. An eagle. Why?"

Sulung: "Because you chose to be more than what your species expected. That deserves protection."

[Trust established — Ally 3 loyal]
```

**TEST C — MORAL QUESTION:**
```
Sulung: "Why defect now? After how long serving?"

Ally 3: [quiet] "There was a child. Pink feathers. Couldn't have been older than 5. 
Crying for her brother. Rajawali ordered me to... silence her."

[Pause — painful memory]

Ally 3: "I couldn't. I refused. That's when I knew — if I stayed, I'd become monster. 
So I fled. Lost everything. Family disowned me. Hunted by both eagles AND those who 
think all eagles are guilty."

Ally 1: [grudging respect] "...I was disowned too. For refusing to serve Eagle Court."

Ally 3: "Then you understand. We're not our species. We're our choices."
```

---

#### Phase 3 — THE DECISION (Recruit or Reject)
**Final Objective:** Decide whether to trust Ally 3 and recruit them
**Narrative Beat:** Allies give input (divided opinions); Sulung makes final call
**Major Choice:** Recruit (gains ally) OR Reject (stays skeptical)
**Emotional Target:** Weighing risk vs reward (can prejudice be overcome?)

**Decision Scene:**
```
[After test, party deliberates]

Ally 1: "I hate to admit it... but they seem genuine. And we need aerial expertise."

Ally 2: "Or it's long con. Eagles are smart. This could be infiltration."

Sulung: [to Ally 3] "If I trust you... and you betray us... my sister dies. Can you live 
with that weight?"

Ally 3: "I've lived with weight of children I DIDN'T save. Saving one would be... start 
of redemption. Not enough. But start."

Sulung: [extends hand] "Redemption has to start somewhere. Welcome to the team."

[OR, if reject:]

Sulung: "I can't risk it. Too much at stake. I'm sorry."

Ally 3: [disappointed but understanding] "I... I get it. Good luck. Truly."

[Ally 3 leaves map/intel as parting gift even if rejected]
```
