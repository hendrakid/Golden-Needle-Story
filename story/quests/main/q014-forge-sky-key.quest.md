# Q014 — FORGING DESTINY: THE SKY KEY & EAGLE COMMANDER

**Type:** Main Quest (ACT 2B FINALE — Boss Fight + Key Item)
**Act:** ACT 2B
**Giver:** Blacksmith contact OR ancient ritual site
**Location:** Sacred Forge (mountain peak) → Eagle Commander Ambush
**Estimated Playtime:** Long (35-45 minutes — crafting + major boss fight)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> All preparation leads to this: forging the Sky Key — magical artifact that grants sustained flight, necessary to reach Pulau Langit (Sky Island). Ancient forge on mountain peak holds power to create it, but requires rare materials gathered across ACT 2. However, eagles have learned of Sulung's plans (thanks to Q011 betrayal). Eagle Commander ambushes at forge. Sulung must defeat major boss AND complete ritual to forge key. Final test before ACT 3.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Determination + Anticipation (almost ready) + Dread (boss approaching)

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Forge Sky Key artifact; gain permanent flight capability
**Real Story:** Final preparation for rescue; last major obstacle before point of no return; proving Sulung has grown from helpless witness to formidable warrior
**Thematic Question:** Have you earned the power to challenge those who wronged you?

#### Phase 2 — THE FORGING RITUAL
**Trigger:** Reach Sacred Forge at mountain summit
**New Objective:** Complete forging ritual (interactive mini-game OR cutscene)
**The Twist:** Mid-ritual, Eagle Commander arrives with elite squad (AMBUSH)
**Emotional Target:** Urgency (must complete ritual while under attack)

**Forging Scene:**
```
[Sacred Forge — ancient stone altar, molten lava pool, magical runes glowing]

[Ancient Blacksmith Spirit OR NPC Blacksmith appears]

Blacksmith: "You bring all components. Rare. Most who seek Sky Key fail to gather even half."

Sulung: [places materials on altar] "I've come too far to fail now."

Blacksmith: "Forging requires time. Ritual must not be interrupted. Can you protect the forge?"

[Ritual begins — magical energies coalesce, materials melting/fusing]

[Suddenly — EAGLE WINGS OVERHEAD]

[Eagle Commander lands with 4 elite guards — blocks exits]

Eagle Commander: "So. The chicken who thinks he can fly. Rajawali sends his regards."

Sulung: [drawing weapon] "Of course he does."

Eagle Commander: "Your mission ends here. Surrender or die."

Ally 1: [if present] "We've fought worse odds."

Eagle Commander: "Have you fought ME?"

[BOSS FIGHT INITIATES]
```

#### Phase 3 — BOSS FIGHT: EAGLE COMMANDER ⚔️🦅
**Final Objective:** Defeat Eagle Commander while protecting forging ritual
**Stakes:** If ritual interrupted (damage to forge), Sky Key creation fails; must restart OR use inferior Flight Scroll (Q011 reward)
**Major Boss Mechanics:** Aerial combat emphasis; player must manage boss AND defend forge
**Emotional Target:** Intensity (hardest boss so far) + Strategic challenge

**BOSS PROFILE — EAGLE COMMANDER:**

**Enemy Stats:**
- **Species:** Eagle (Elite Military Officer)
- **Name:** Commander Garuda OR "The Iron Talon"
- **Personality:** Professional soldier; follows orders without question; views Sulung as threat to Eagle superiority
- **Motivation:** Duty to Rajawali; belief in eagle supremacy; career advancement

**BOSS MECHANICS (3 Phases):**

**PHASE 1 — GROUND ASSAULT (100%-70% HP):**
```
Commander: "Let's see if training ground chickens to fight was waste of time."

Mechanics:
- Dual talon strikes (fast melee combo)
- Wing shield (blocks frontal attacks, must flank)
- Tactical commands (elite guards attack in formation)
- Occasional aerial reposition (leaps back, surveys battlefield)

Player Objectives:
- Defeat/distract 4 elite guards (OR allies handle them)
- Damage Commander while protecting forge from guards
- Learn attack patterns (telegraphed but punishing)

Mid-Phase Dialogue:
Commander: "You fight well. For prey. But prey you remain."
Sulung: "I'm done being prey."
```

**PHASE 2 — AERIAL DOMINANCE (70%-40% HP):**
```
[Commander takes flight fully]

Commander: "Enough ground games. SKY is where you lose!"

[If Ally 1 or Ally 3 present, they engage in aerial dogfight]

Mechanics:
- Dive bomb attacks (must dodge/counter)
- Wing blade tornadoes (AoE, destroys cover)
- Aerial grab (if caught, Sulung thrown OR dropped)
- **FORGE THREAT:** Commander targets ritual itself (must intercept)

Player Objectives:
- Dodge aerial attacks (learn timing)
- Use environment (pillars, forge structure for cover)
- Protect forge (intercept Commander when targeting ritual)
- Deal damage during landing recovery windows

Critical Moment:
[Commander dives toward forge]
Commander: "If I can't kill you, I'll destroy what you're making!"
[PLAYER MUST INTERCEPT — QTE or timed action]
Sulung: [leaps, blocks] "NOT TODAY!"
```

**PHASE 3 — DESPERATION (40%-0% HP):**
```
[Commander wounded, landing, fighting on ground again but more aggressive]

Commander: "I won't fail Lord Rajawali! I WON'T!"

Mechanics:
- Berserker mode (faster, stronger, less defense)
- Bleed attacks (damage over time)
- Kamikaze dive (sacrificial attack if near death)
- Elite guards revive (if not fully defeated earlier)

Player Objectives:
- Exploit Commander's recklessness (openings larger but windows shorter)
- Finish fight before ritual completes (time pressure both ways)
- Survive kamikaze attack if triggered

Final Exchange:
Commander: [defeated, on knees] "How... you're just... chicken..."
Sulung: "I'm a chicken who has something to fight for. You're just following orders."
Commander: [collapses]

[Ritual completes — SKY KEY manifests in bright light]
```

**World Change:** Ready for Sky Island assault; final preparation complete

**Echo Forward:** Sky Key essential for Q016-Q020; symbolizes Sulung's transformation

**Victory Scene:**
```

Blacksmith: "It is done. The Sky Key."

truth and resolve. You are ready."

```
