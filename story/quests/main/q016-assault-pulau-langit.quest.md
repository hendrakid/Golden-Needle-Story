# Q016 — ASSAULT ON PULAU LANGIT: BRIEF HOPE, THEN HORROR

**Type:** Main Quest
**Act:** ACT 3
**Giver:** Self-motivated (continuation from Q015 Point of No Return)
**Location:** Pulau Langit (Sky Island Fortress) — Exterior → Interior Infiltration
**Estimated Playtime:** Medium (20-30 minutes)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> After committing to stop Rajawali, Sulung and allies (if recruited) reach the Sky Island. The floating fortress looms ahead — clouds swirl around its base, eagle guards patrol the perimeter. This is it. Bungsu is somewhere inside those walls.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Player has been working toward this moment since Scene 01. Finally, rescue seems within reach.

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Infiltrate Pulau Langit, find Bungsu, rescue her before Rajawali discovers intrusion
**Real Story:** A brief moment of hope before the story's darkest tragedy — Sulung WILL find Bungsu alive, but fate has other plans
**Thematic Question:** Can determination overcome impossible odds? (Answer in this quest: No. Not yet. Not without cost.)

---

### 🗺️ Quest Structure

#### Phase 1 — APPROACH & INFILTRATION
**Objective:** Reach Sky Island and choose entry method
**Location:** Airspace approaching Pulau Langit
**Narrative Beat:** Sulung flies toward island using temporary flight solution (Ally 3 carrying, magic scroll from Q011, or Sky Key fragment magic); sees fortress up close for first time; scale is overwhelming
**Player Discovery:** Sky Island is MASSIVE — multiple levels, guard towers, central throne room visible at peak
**Emotional Target:** Awe mixed with dread

**Gameplay:**
- **Flight Approach:** If Sky Key from Q014 → sustained flight; If magic scroll from Q011 → limited duration flight; If neither → cutscene solution (allies help from below)
- **Entry Choice:**
  - **Stealth Route:** Servants' entrance, avoid guards, slower but safer (Licik excels here if recruited)
  - **Combat Route:** Front gate assault, loud but direct
- **Consequence:** Choice affects Phase 2 timing (stealth = more time with Bungsu; combat = Rajawali arrives faster)

---

#### Phase 2 — SEARCH & DISCOVERY
**Trigger:** Successfully enter fortress interior
**New Objective:** Navigate fortress, locate prisoner holding area
**The Twist/Reveal:** Multiple cages found — OTHER kidnapped chickens imprisoned here (not just Bungsu); systematic operation revealed
**Player Choice (if any):** Free other prisoners now OR prioritize Bungsu first
**Emotional Target:** Horror (scale of Rajawali's crimes) + Urgency (must find Bungsu)

**Gameplay:**
- **Exploration:** Navigate multi-level fortress (platforming if flight available, stealth if grounded)
- **Environmental Storytelling:** See cages, eagle propaganda posters, opulent throne room contrasted with dungeon squalor
- **Optional Objective:** Free 2-3 other captive chickens (if stealth route + time permitting) — they provide info on Bungsu's location

**Key Discovery:**
- **Pusaka Artifact** visible in throne room display case — PROOF of Rajawali's theft (player sees it but cannot reach yet)
- **Bungsu's Location:** Top floor, isolated cage near throne room (Rajawali keeps "newest acquisition" close)

---

#### Phase 3 — THE REUNION (Bittersweet)
**Final Objective:** Reach Bungsu's cage, attempt rescue
**Stakes:** Time is running out — guards will notice intrusion soon
**Major Choice (if applicable):** N/A (linear narrative beat for maximum emotional impact)
**Emotional Target:** HOPE → JOY → DREAD (emotional whiplash)

**Cutscene — Finding Bungsu:**
```
[Sulung enters chamber, sees cage in corner]

[Small figure with PINK FEATHERS huddled inside]

Sulung: [whispers] "Bungsu...?"

[Bungsu looks up — eyes widen in recognition]

Bungsu: [weak voice] "...Kakak?"

[Sulung rushes to cage, grabs bars]

Sulung: "I'm here. I came for you. I'm getting you out—"

Bungsu: [tears streaming] "Kak... you can... you can fly now?!" [noticed he flew here]

Sulung: [fumbling with lock] "I'll explain later. We need to—"

[Lock opens — Sulung reaches in, pulls Bungsu into embrace]

[BRIEF JOYFUL REUNION — they hug, Bungsu crying, Sulung comforting]

Bungsu: "I knew you'd come. I knew..."

Sulung: "We're leaving. Right now. Can you walk?"

Bungsu: [stands shakily] "I think so—"

[DISTANT SOUND — heavy footsteps, wings flapping]

[Chamber doors SLAM OPEN]

[RAJAWALI enters, massive, blocking exit]

Rajawali: "How touching."

[Sulung instinctively pushes Bungsu behind him]

Rajawali: "Did you really think it would be this easy, little chicken?"

[Eagle guards pour in, surround them]

Rajawali: [to guards] "Separate them."

[Guards grab Bungsu, pull her away from Sulung despite his resistance]

Bungsu: "KAK! KAKAK!"

Sulung: [fighting guards] "LET HER GO!"

Rajawali: "Let's see if you're worthy of challenging me."

[Cutscene transitions to Q017 boss fight trigger]
```

#### Phase 4 — SEPARATION & SETUP FOR TRAGEDY
**Outcome:** Bungsu torn away from Sulung; Rajawali forces confrontation
**World Change:** Hope shattered — rescue failed before it began
**Character Change:** Sulung from relieved → desperate → cornered
**Echo Forward:** This separation is the last time Sulung sees Bungsu ALIVE (though he doesn't know it yet)
