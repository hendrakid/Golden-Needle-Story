# Q019 — RETURN TO THE SKY: WINGS OF SORROW

**Type:** Main Quest
**Act:** ACT 3
**Giver:** Self-motivated (with Bungsu spirit's encouragement)
**Location:** Journey from Riverside Sanctuary → Pulau Langit Approach
**Estimated Playtime:** Medium (25-35 minutes)

---

### 🦅 **CRITICAL DESIGN NOTE**

**THIS QUEST IS PREPARATION & EMPOWERMENT:**
- **Flight Tutorial:** Player learns to fly using Sayap Emas (powered by Bungsu's soul)
- **Bungsu as Active Companion:** She guides, encourages, and dialogues throughout
- **Reunite with Allies (if any):** Escaped/freed allies rejoin OR Sulung goes solo
- **Choose Final Approach:** Stealth/Diplomatic/Direct assault on Pulau Langit
- **Emotional Tone:** Determination mixed with bittersweetness (flying with dead sister's power)

**Player Experience Goals:**
- Mastery of flight mechanics (prepare for Q020 aerial combat)
- Bond with Bungsu's spirit (establish her as permanent companion)
- Strategic choice (how to approach final confrontation)
- Calm before storm (preparation montage energy before climax)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> Sulung stands at cliff edge, Sayap Emas glowing on his back. Bungsu's spirit floats beside him, pink and radiant. Together, they will return to the place where she died. This time, Sulung can fly. This time, he's ready.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Determination + Bittersweet Hope (we can do this together... even though you're gone)

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Learn to fly, return to Pulau Langit, prepare for final confrontation with Rajawali
**Real Story:** Brother and sister (living and spirit) working together as one; reclaiming the sky that Rajawali claimed chickens could never reach
**Thematic Question:** Can the powerless become powerful through love and sacrifice? (Answer: Yes. Sulung flies using Bungsu's soul — ultimate collaboration.)

---

### 🗺️ Quest Structure

#### Phase 1 — FIRST FLIGHT (Tutorial with Bungsu) ✨
**Objective:** Learn to fly using Sayap Emas
**Location:** Cliff near riverside sanctuary → open sky
**Narrative Beat:** Bungsu teaches Sulung how to fly; emotional scene (she always wanted to fly, now she can through him)
**Player Discovery:** Flight feels natural when Bungsu guides; their bond makes wings work
**Emotional Target:** Joy mixed with sadness (this should be her flying, not through me)

**Flight Tutorial Cutscene:**
```
[Sulung at cliff edge, looking down at valley below]

Sulung: "I've never flown before... What if I fall?"

Bungsu (spirit): [floating beside him, excited] "You won't fall, Kak! I'm here with you!"

Sulung: "But you're the one making the wings work. What if—"

Bungsu: "Then we'll figure it out together! Come on, I've always wanted to fly!"

[She floats in front of him, facing him, glowing brighter]

Bungsu: "Close your eyes, Kak. Feel me. In here." [gestures to his chest]

[Sulung closes eyes, places hand on chest]

Sulung: [whispers] "I feel you..."

Bungsu: "Now... think about going up. We'll do it together."

[Sayap Emas begins to GLOW — golden-pink light]

[Wings extend — beautiful, majestic]

Bungsu: "Open your eyes, Kak!"

[Sulung opens eyes — feet lifting off ground]

Sulung: [gasps] "We're... we're flying!"

Bungsu: [laughing, spinning around him in the air] "WE'RE FLYING, KAK!"

[They rise together — camera pulls back to show them ascending into sky]

[Music: Triumphant yet melancholic (bittersweet victory theme)]
```

**Gameplay — Flight Tutorial:**
- **Basic Controls:** Ascend, descend, forward movement, turning
- **Bungsu Guidance:** She provides tips ("Tilt left, Kak!" "Faster! Like this!" "Careful, that cloud looks heavy!")
- **Safe Environment:** Open sky, no enemies, forgiving mechanics
- **Visual:** Pink energy trail follows Sulung when flying (Bungsu's presence)
- **Tutorial Objectives:**
  - Fly through 5 floating rings
  - Practice aerial turns
  - Dive and recover
  - Land safely on designated platform

**Emotional Dialogue During Flight:**
```
Bungsu: "Isn't it beautiful, Kak? The sky... it's so big!"
Sulung: "You should be the one flying. Not me. Not like this."
Bungsu: "But I AM flying, Kak. Through you. With you. This is perfect."
Sulung: [quiet] "I wish you could feel the wind on your feathers."
Bungsu: "I do feel it, Kak. I feel everything you feel. We're one now."
```

#### Phase 2 — REUNION & PREPARATION
**Trigger:** Complete flight tutorial; Bungsu suggests finding allies
**New Objective:** Reunite with allies (if recruited in ACT 2) OR proceed solo
**The Twist/Reveal:** Allies escaped Pulau Langit OR were never captured (depends on Q016 choice)
**Player Choice:** Wait for allies OR go solo immediately
**Emotional Target:** Camaraderie (we're not alone) OR Resolve (I'll do this myself if I must)

**Ally Reunion Scene (IF Allies Exist):**
```
[Sulung lands at pre-arranged meeting point]

Taji (Ajag archer — if recruited): "Sulung! You're alive! We thought—"

Sulung: "I'm alive. Barely. But I'm back."

Senja (Musang mage — if recruited): [notices Sulung's new wings] "Those wings... where did you get—"

[Sulung glances at Bungsu's spirit floating beside him — Allies DON'T see her]

Sulung: "It's... a long story. Bungsu is— she didn't make it."

Allies: [react with sadness, offer condolences]

Sulung: "But she's still with me. In a way. And we're going back. To end this."

Licik (Rubah assassin — if recruited): "Rajawali will be ready for you. He knows you'll return."

Sulung: "Good. I want him to know I'm coming."

Bungsu (spirit — only Sulung hears): [whispers] "Kak, they can't see me, can they?"

Sulung: [subtle head shake — others don't notice]

Bungsu: "That's okay. As long as you can."
```

**IF NO ALLIES:**
```
Sulung: [alone on clifftop, Bungsu (spirit) beside him]

Sulung: "Just us, then. Like it's always been."

Bungsu: "We don't need anyone else, Kak. We have each other."

Sulung: "Is that enough?"

Bungsu: "It has to be."
```

---

#### Phase 3 — STRATEGIC CHOICE (Approach Selection)
**Final Objective:** Decide how to assault Pulau Langit for rematch
**Stakes:** Final approach affects Q020 difficulty and available cutscenes
**Major Choice:** Choose assault strategy
**Emotional Target:** Determination (this is it — no turning back)

**Choice Presentation:**
```
Sulung: [looking at Sky Island in distance] "How do we do this?"

Bungsu: "What do you mean?"

Sulung: "Rajawali knows I'm coming. He'll be ready. Do we sneak in? Fight our way through? Or..."

[Player presented with 3 options:]

1. STEALTH APPROACH
   - Infiltrate via servants' passages (like Q016 stealth route)
   - Avoid guards, reach throne room quietly
   - Allies (if any) create distraction outside
   - Bungsu helps scout (spirit can pass through walls, report positions)
   - **Outcome:** Reach Rajawali with element of surprise, fewer guards in final fight

2. DIPLOMATIC UPRISING
   - Rally freed prisoners from Q016 (if freed)
   - Licik (if Rubah assassin recruited) spreads truth among guards, uses street connections
   - Create internal rebellion against Rajawali
   - Public confrontation instead of private duel
   - **Outcome:** Witnesses present during Q020 fight, easier to expose truth publicly

3. DIRECT ASSAULT
   - Fly straight to throne room, announce presence
   - Challenge Rajawali openly (honor duel)
   - Allies (if any) fight through guards while Sulung goes to throne room
   - **Outcome:** Epic confrontation, harder fight but most dramatic

[Bungsu provides input for each option if asked]

Bungsu (for Stealth): "Remember, Kak, you're faster now. We can fly past them."
Bungsu (for Diplomatic): "If we show others the truth, Rajawali loses his power over them."
Bungsu (for Direct): "Sometimes the bravest thing is to face your fear head-on."

[Sulung chooses — locks in approach for Q020]
```

#### Phase 4 — THE FLIGHT TO DESTINY
**Outcome:** Fly toward Pulau Langit with chosen approach locked
**World Change:** Sky Island now accessible (no barriers with flight)
**Character Change:** Sulung fully embraces role as avenger/justice-seeker
**Echo Forward:** Approach choice affects Q020 opening

**Final Scene Before Q020:**
```
[Sulung flying toward Pulau Langit, Bungsu (spirit) flying alongside]

[Camera: Wide aerial shot — tiny figures approaching massive floating fortress]

Bungsu: "Kak... are you scared?"

Sulung: "Terrified."

Bungsu: "Me too. But we'll be okay. Together."

Sulung: "He killed you, Bungsu. Right in front of me. I can't let that stand."

Bungsu: "I know. But promise me something?"

Sulung: "What?"

Bungsu: "Don't let hate consume you. Remember why we're doing this. For justice. For all of them. Not just revenge."

Sulung: [quiet] "I'll try."

Bungsu: "That's all I ask."

[They fly in silence toward fortress]

[Music builds — approaching climax]

[Fade to white as they enter Sky Island airspace]

[Text: "The Final Confrontation"]

[Transition to Q020]
```
