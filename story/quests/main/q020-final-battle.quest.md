# Q020 — THE FINAL BATTLE: VENGEANCE WITH A GHOST

**Type:** Main Quest (FINALE)
**Act:** ACT 3
**Giver:** Self-motivated (culmination of entire journey)
**Location:** Pulau Langit — Throne Room
**Estimated Playtime:** Epic (45-60 minutes including boss fight + ending variations)

---

### ⚔️👻 **CRITICAL DESIGN NOTE**

**THIS IS THE FINAL QUEST — STORY CLIMAX:**
- **Rematch with Rajawali** — this time Sulung has flight (powered by Bungsu's soul)
- **Bungsu actively participates** as jiwa peri (dialogue, encouragement, guidance)
- **Public truth revelation** — witnesses see/hear Rajawali's confession
- **Player's final moral choice** — Kill / Capture / Spare / Offer Redemption
- **Multiple Endings** triggered by choice (A, B, C1, C2, D, E variants)
- **NO "SAVE BUNGSU" OPTION** — she's permanently dead; endings are about living with her spirit

**Player Experience Goals:**
- Cathartic boss fight (avenge Bungsu with her help)
- Moral choice that matters (player decides Rajawali's fate)
- Narrative closure (all story threads resolved)
- Emotional payoff (bittersweet endings appropriate to dark version)
- Companion bond (Bungsu's spirit integral to victory)

---

### 🎯 Quest Hook
> Sulung and Bungsu (spirit) stand before Rajawali's throne room doors. This is it. The place where she died. The place where Sulung failed. But he's not the same chicken who fell from this fortress. He can fly now. He has truth. And he has Bungsu with him—always.

**Emotional Entry Point:** Determination + Righteous Anger + Bungsu's Calming Presence

---

### 📖 Quest Premise
**Surface Goal:** Defeat Rajawali in combat, expose truth about legend, determine his fate
**Real Story:** Brother and sister (living and spirit) confronting the monster who tore them apart; choosing how justice looks when the dead cannot return
**Thematic Question:** How do we honor the dead—through vengeance, justice, or mercy? (Answer: Player decides.)

```

Bungsu: [spirit floating aggressively] "KAK, HAJAR DIA!"
```

#### Phase 2 — BOSS FIGHT (Three Phases) ⚔️

**PHASE 1 — GROUND COMBAT (Equal Footing)**

**Setup:**
- Both combatants on ground initially
- Rajawali tests Sulung's improvement
- Bungsu provides encouragement

**Mechanics:**
- Standard melee combat (sword vs talon)
- Rajawali's attacks: Talon swipes, wing buffet, dive grab
- **NEW:** Sulung can dodge-roll, parry, counter (improved from Q017)
- **Bungsu Assistance:** Calls out attack warnings ("Kak, he's diving!" "Left side!")

**Mid-Phase Dialogue:**
```
Rajawali: [after Sulung lands solid hit] "You've improved. Good. It would be boring otherwise."

Sulung: "I trained for this. I'm not the same chicken you threw off this island."

Rajawali: [attacks] "Training won't save you!"

Bungsu: [spirit] "Block, Kak! NOW!"

[If Sulung follows her warning, successful parry triggers]

Rajawali: [frustrated] "Stop hearing things that aren't there!"

Sulung: "She IS here. You just can't see her."
```

**Phase Transition Trigger:** Reduce Rajawali to 70% HP

---

**PHASE 2 — AERIAL DUEL (Flight Equality) 🦅**

**Setup:**
```
Rajawali: [takes flight] "Let's see if your borrowed wings can keep up!"

[Rajawali ascends to throne room ceiling, circling]

Sulung: [activates Sayap Emas — golden-pink glow intensifies]

Bungsu: [spirit flying alongside, excited] "Kak, we can fly together! Show him!"

[Sulung takes flight — FIRST AERIAL COMBAT]

Rajawali: [shocked seeing Sulung airborne] "IMPOSSIBLE! Chickens cannot—"

Sulung: [mid-air] "I have what you never will. Someone who believes in me."

[Bungsu's spirit becomes MORE visible during flight — pink energy trails]
```

**Mechanics:**
- **3D Aerial Combat:** Dive attacks, mid-air dodges, aerial combos
- **Bungsu's Role:** Her spirit guides Sulung's movements ("Dive now, Kak!" "Barrel roll!")
- **Visual:** Pink spirit energy trails when Sulung maneuvers; Bungsu appears to "dance" around him
- **Rajawali's Attacks:** 
  - Aerial tackle (fast, requires dodge timing)
  - Wing blade slash (swings wings like weapons)
  - Sky drop (grabs Sulung, attempts to slam him down)

**Mid-Phase Revelation:**
```
[During aerial clash, both fighters grapple mid-air]

Rajawali: [straining] "Where... did you get... those wings?!"

Sulung: "From your brother. The eagle who OPPOSED your coup!"

Rajawali: [eyes widen] "My... brother? He's alive?!"

Sulung: "He's alive. And he told me EVERYTHING. The truth you've hidden!"

[They break apart, circle each other in air]

Rajawali: [rage building] "My brother was a FOOL who couldn't see necessity!"

Sulung: "Your brother was honorable. You're the monster."
```

**Phase Transition Trigger:** Reduce Rajawali to 40% HP

---

**PHASE 3 — DESPERATION & TRUTH EXPOSED (Public Confession)**

**Setup:**
```
[Rajawali wounded, lands in throne room center]

[If Diplomatic Approach: Crowd of witnesses watching from doorways]

[If Stealth/Direct: Guards have gathered, hearing the commotion]

Rajawali: [breathing hard, moves to throne, grabs PUSAKA ARTIFACT from display]

Sulung: [lands, ready] "Using that won't save you."

Rajawali: [holding pusaka] "This is MY RIGHT! I EARNED this!"

Sulung: [points at pusaka] "You STOLE it! From your own people!"

[Witnesses react — murmurs, gasps]

Eagle Guard: [confused] "My lord... what is he talking about?"

Rajawali: [cornered] "LIES! The chicken clan—"

Sulung: [shouts to witnesses] "There was NO chicken thief! There was NO perjanjian darah!"

[Crowd silences, listening]

Sulung: "Rajawali staged a coup! He murdered the old eagle council! He stole that pusaka to seize power!"

Rajawali: "Enough!"

Bungsu: [spirit, to Sulung] "Tell them about the pink feathers, Kak!"

Sulung: "The 'tradition' of kidnapping pink-feathered chickens? He INVENTED it! Pink and yellow mean NOTHING! It's arbitrary cruelty disguised as ancient law!"

[More shocked murmurs from witnesses]

Freed Chicken (if Diplomatic): "My daughter had pink feathers... He took her... She never came back..."

Sulung: "They never come back. Because he MURDERS them. All of them."

Rajawali: [cracking under pressure] "They were NECESSARY! To maintain ORDER! To keep chickens AFRAID!"

[Crowd recoils — Rajawali just confessed]

Eagle Guard: "My lord... did you just admit...?"

Rajawali: [realizes mistake, snarls] "I did what I had to do! For eagle supremacy! For—"

Bungsu: [spirit, gently] "He's confessing, Kak. They're all hearing it."

Sulung: [to Rajawali] "You killed innocent children. You lied for decades. You murdered your own family. And for what? Power?"

Rajawali: [desperate rage] "YES! POWER! And I'll KILL YOU TOO!"

[Activates pusaka OR summons last of strength for final attack]
```

**Final Combat:**
- Rajawali empowered by pusaka (temporary strength boost) OR pure desperation
- Last stand mechanics (more aggressive, reckless attacks)
- **Bungsu's Critical Role:** 
  ```
  Bungsu: "Kak! The pusaka's glow! Aim for it!"
  
  [If player targets pusaka, it shatters — Rajawali loses power boost]
  
  OR
  
  Bungsu: "Together, Kak! Like we practiced!"
  
  [Combo finisher: Sulung + Bungsu spirit energy fusion attack]
  ```

**Victory Condition:** Reduce Rajawali to 0% HP
