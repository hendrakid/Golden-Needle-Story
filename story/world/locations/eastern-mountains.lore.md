# Eastern Mountains (Pegunungan Timur)

**Region Type:** Mountain Range / Prohibited Zone  
**Access Level:** Restricted (Eagle patrols enforce "no entry" policy)  
**Climate:** Alpine, cold winds, sudden storms at high altitude  
**Danger Level:** High (wildlife, terrain, eagle patrols)

---

## Overview

The **Eastern Mountains** are a vast, rugged mountain range that stretches along the eastern border of known territories. Officially designated as a "prohibited zone" by the Neutral Council (under pressure from eagle authorities), these peaks are largely unexplored by modern civilization.

However, ancient records suggest the mountains once served as neutral ground and trade routes between ground-dwelling and aerial species. Hidden among the peaks are **ancient ruins** — remnants of the pre-conflict era when chickens and eagles cooperated.

---

## Geography & Features

### Mountain Peaks
- **Mount Awan Tinggi** (Highest Peak): Altitude ~12,000 feet; perpetually shrouded in clouds
- **Twin Fangs Ridge**: Sharp dual peaks marking the northern boundary
- **Sunrise Plateau**: Eastern slope where dawn light creates unique atmospheric conditions

### Natural Phenomena
- **Dawn Updrafts**: Powerful vertical air currents generated at sunrise due to temperature differentials; strong enough to lift large objects to high altitude
- **Floating Shadows**: Massive shapes visible at dawn when sunlight hits them at specific angles — later revealed to be Sky Island (Pulau Langit) floating above the peaks
- **Storm Convergence**: Seasonal wind patterns create perpetual storm layer between 7,000-9,000 feet, obscuring higher altitudes

### Landmarks
- **Ancient Temple Ruins** (Location: Hidden valley, northwestern quadrant) — Pre-conflict sanctuary with historical murals and map fragments (explored in Q008)
- **Crystal Caves** (Location: Mid-altitude, southeast face) — Natural crystalline formations with magical properties
- **Abandoned Watchtowers** (Multiple locations) — Old observation posts from when mountains were patrolled by mixed-species peacekeepers

## Historical Significance

### Pre-Conflict Era (400+ years ago)
The Eastern Mountains served as:
- **Trade Routes**: Mountain passes connected ground settlements to aerial territories
- **Pilgrimage Sites**: Sacred temples where mixed-species councils convened
- **Sky Key Forges**: Ritual sites where flight-enabling artifacts were created for ground species to attend councils on Sky Island

### Rajawali's Prohibition (Modern Era)
After seizing control of Sky Island, Rajawali's faction:
- **Declared mountains "off-limits"** to prevent discovery of ancient ruins containing historical truth
- **Stationed eagle patrols** to enforce prohibition
- **Spread rumors** of deadly monsters and cursed grounds to deter explorers
- **Suppressed knowledge** of the mountains' connection to Sky Island's location

**Truth**: The prohibition exists to hide evidence that the Chicken-Eagle Legend is fabricated.

## Current State (ACT 2A)

### Access & Restrictions
- **Official Status**: Prohibited Zone (Council decree under eagle pressure)
- **Patrol Frequency**: Eagle squads fly perimeter every 6 hours
- **Enforcement**: Trespassers detained or killed on sight (no trial)
- **Exceptions**: None officially; smugglers use hidden routes illegally

### Wildlife & Dangers
- **Predators**: Mountain wolves, snow leopards, territorial raptors (non-sapient)
- **Environment**: Rockslides, avalanches, sudden temperature drops
- **Magical Anomalies**: Residual ancient magic causes disorientation in certain areas

### Hidden Communities
- **Hermits & Exiles**: Small population of outcasts living in caves, avoiding authorities
- **Smuggler Caches**: Black market networks maintain hidden supply depots
- **Nomadic Tribes**: Taji's former wild dog clan occasionally passes through (rare)

## Trivia & Secrets

- The **Twin Fangs Ridge** was named after two eagle siblings who died defending the peaks during an ancient conflict (predates fabricated legend)
- **Crystal Caves** are rumored to be tears of a sleeping earth spirit; scholars debate if this is myth or magical reality
- Taji's birth pack still roams these mountains seasonally, though he avoids encountering them
- A hidden **Sky Key Forge** exists on the Sacred Forge peak (northeast of main range) — location known only to Scholar Malik and ancient texts
