# Pulau Langit (Sky Island)

**Ancient Name:** Sanctuary of Unity  
**Current Name:** Pulau Langit (Sky Island)  
**Ruler:** Lord Rajawali (Eagle Tyrant)  
**Location:** Floating 8,000-10,000 feet above Mount Awan Tinggi, Eastern Mountain Range  
**Population:** ~500 eagles + prisoners  
**Status:** Fortress, Prison, Symbol of Eagle Supremacy

---

## Overview

**Pulau Langit** is a massive floating island held aloft by ancient magic — once a neutral meeting ground where all species convened in peace, now transformed into an impenetrable fortress and the heart of Rajawali's authoritarian regime.

The island represents the ultimate corruption: a place built for **unity** weaponized for **oppression**.

---

## Physical Structure & Geography

### The Island Itself

**Size**: Approximately 2 square miles (irregular shape)  
**Altitude**: 8,000-10,000 feet ASL (above sea level); floats due to ancient levitation magic  
**Position**: Drifts slightly with seasonal winds but anchored above Mount Awan Tinggi  
**Visibility**: Obscured by perpetual storm layer (7,000-9,000 feet); only visible from specific angles at dawn

**Natural Features**:
- **Central Plateau**: Flat ceremonial grounds (where unity councils once met)
- **Northern Cliffs**: Sheer drops; natural defense
- **Southern Forest**: Small wooded area with ancient trees (predates Rajawali)
- **Crystal Springs**: Magical water sources providing sustenance
- **Sky Gardens**: Terraced gardens maintained by prisoners (forced labor)

### Architectural Zones

#### THE GREAT HALL (Central)
- **Original Purpose**: Council chamber for inter-species diplomacy
- **Current Use**: Rajawali's throne room and propaganda center
- **Architecture**: Vaulted ceiling showing murals of eagle "victories" (painted over original unity mosaics)
- **The Throne**: Carved from single massive crystal; Rajawali holds court here
- **Blood Altar**: Where "covenant ceremonies" occur (Bungsu's fate was meant to be here)

#### NORTH TOWER COMPLEX
- **Tower Block D**: **PRISONER CELLS** (Bungsu held here, Level 3)
  - 5-level tower, cells on levels 2-4
  - Small barred windows facing outward (psychological torment: can see freedom, can't reach it)
  - Heavy guards, magical locks
  - **Bungsu's Cell**: Northwest corner, Level 3, Cell #17
  
- **Guard Barracks**: Housing for 200+ eagle soldiers
- **Armory**: Weapons, armor, siege equipment
- **Training Grounds**: Combat drills, aerial maneuvers

#### SOUTH TOWER (Administrative)
- **Archive Wing**: Records of all prisoners, genealogies, fabricated histories
- **Officer Quarters**: High-ranking eagle commanders
- **Observatory**: Used to monitor ground territories and incoming threats
- **Council Chambers** (unused): Old meeting rooms, now storage

#### EAST WING — The Forges
- **Weapon Smithing**: Producing eagle military equipment
- **Magical Workshops**: Enchantment of items, ward maintenance
- **Power Core Chamber**: Heart of island's levitation magic (ANCIENT, pre-Rajawali)

#### WEST WING — Living Quarters
- **Rajawali's Palace**: Opulent private chambers
- **Servants' Quarters**: Non-eagle species forced into servitude
- **Kitchens & Storage**: Food preparation (supplies brought from ground)

#### COURTYARDS & OPEN AREAS
- **North Courtyard**: Main landing zone for incoming eagles (Q016 assault entry point)
- **Execution Plaza**: Where "traitors" to tradition are killed publicly
- **Memorial Garden**: Propaganda site claiming to honor "victims of chicken theft" (fabricated history)

## Magical Properties

### Levitation System

**Original Design** (400+ years ago):
- Ancient mages from all species collaborated to create floating sanctuary
- Powered by **Unity Crystals** embedded throughout island
- Magic draws from collective goodwill and cooperation (philosophical magic)
- Self-sustaining as long as purpose remained peaceful

**Current State** (under Rajawali):
- Unity Crystals are CORRUPTED (still functional but tainted by oppression)
- Magic sustains island through **fear and dominance** instead of cooperation
- Rajawali doesn't understand original system; accidentally maintains it through tyranny's "strength"
- **Vulnerability**: If Unity Crystals are destroyed, island falls

### Defensive Wards

**Aerial Approach Barriers**:
- Detection wards alert guards to flying intruders within 15-mile radius
- Wind magic creates turbulence at specific altitudes (makes approach difficult)
- Illusion layers hide island from most angles (only visible at dawn from eastern mountains)

**Magical Locks**:
- Prisoner cells use blood-key locks (only eagle talon can open — ironic)
- Great Hall entrance requires Rajawali's personal authorization
- Power Core Chamber sealed with ancient locks (keys lost to history)

**Weaknesses**:
- Dawn approach during shift change (guards changing, wards briefly recalibrating)
- Southern tower has blind spot in detection grid (architectural flaw Rajawali never fixed)
- Maintenance tunnels under courtyards not warded (original builders trusted Unity)

## Society & Culture

### Eagle Hierarchy

**LORD RAJAWALI** (Supreme Ruler):
- Absolute authority
- Lives in West Wing palace
- Makes all major decisions
- Seen as semi-divine by loyalists

**INNER CIRCLE** (Advisors):
- 5 generals commanding military operations
- High Priest (conducts "covenant ceremonies")
- Spymaster (manages ground infiltration networks)

**MILITARY** (200+ soldiers):
- Elite Sky Guard (personal protection for Rajawali)
- Border Patrol (enforcement of restricted airspace)
- Prison Guards (oversee captives)
- Rank-and-file (general soldiers)

**CIVILIANS** (~300 eagles):
- Artisans, scholars, merchants
- Families of military personnel
- Live in East Wing residential zones
- Mostly indoctrinated; genuinely believe legend

### Prisoner System

**Categories of Captives**:

1. **Covenant Prisoners** (Pink-feathered chickens):
   - "Legally" held under fabricated perjanjian
   - Kept in North Tower cells
   - Fate: Ceremonial sacrifice OR forced servitude until death
   - Current count: 47 (including Bungsu)

2. **Political Prisoners**:
   - Dissident eagles who questioned Rajawali
   - Rivals to throne
   - Kept in underground dungeons (harsher conditions than "covenant" prisoners)

3. **Servants** (forced labor):
   - Non-eagle species captured during raids
   - Work in kitchens, gardens, maintenance
   - Slightly better treatment than prisoners but still slaves

### Daily Life on Island

**For Eagles**:
- Comfortable existence (food, shelter, purpose)
- Propaganda constantly reinforces supremacy myth
- Children taught fabricated history from birth
- Dissent is UNTHINKABLE (those who question disappear)

**For Prisoners**:
- Minimal food (just enough to survive)
- Forced labor in gardens/kitchens
- Psychological torture (told they're "honored" to fulfill covenant)
- No hope of escape (island is unreachable without flight)

## Historical Transformation

### Original Purpose (400-150 years ago)

**Sanctuary of Unity**:
- Neutral diplomatic hub
- All species sent representatives for councils
- Conflict resolution through dialogue
- Symbol of cooperation overcoming division
- Sky Keys allowed ground species to visit

**Notable Achievements**:
- Treaty of 412 RE (ended ancient chicken-eagle war)
- Trade agreements creating prosperity
- Cultural exchange programs
- Mixed-species scholarship

### Rajawali's Coup (~150 years ago)

**How Rajawali Seized Control**:
1. Infiltrated Unity Council as "reform candidate"
2. Gradually placed loyalists in key positions
3. Staged false flag attack blamed on ground species
4. Used crisis to justify "emergency powers"
5. Purged dissenters (killed or exiled Good Eagle's faction)
6. Rewrote history to justify new regime

**Post-Coup Changes**:
- Destroyed all Sky Keys (prevent ground species from visiting)
- Sealed historical archives (hide truth)
- Created fabricated legend to legitimize kidnappings
- Transformed Great Hall from council chamber to throne room
- Militarized society (every eagle serves regime)

### Current Era (Present Day)

- Three generations of eagles born under propaganda
- Original purpose of island nearly forgotten
- Ground species believe island is ancient eagle homeland (lie)
- System self-perpetuates through indoctrination

## Strategic Importance

### Why Rajawali Needs Sky Island

1. **Physical Security**: Unreachable by most ground species = safe power base
2. **Psychological Control**: Floating fortress inspires awe/fear
3. **Resource Independence**: Self-sustaining (food gardens, water springs)
4. **Symbolic Power**: Literally "above" other species = supremacy metaphor
5. **Historical Legitimacy**: Controlling Sanctuary gives claim to "ancient authority"

### Why Ground Species Can't Easily Attack

- **Altitude**: 8,000+ feet; impossible to reach without flight magic
- **Detection**: 15-mile early warning system
- **Aerial Superiority**: Eagles can fly; ground forces can't
- **Supply Lines**: No need for external trade (gardens produce food)
- **Natural Defenses**: Cliffs, storms, magical barriers

**Exception**: With Sky Key, Sulung's party can breach defenses (Q016)

## Role in Story (ACT 3)

### Q016 — Assault on Pulau Langit

**Entry Strategy**:
- Dawn approach using Eastern Mountain updrafts
- Sky Key provides sustained flight
- Land in North Courtyard (less guarded than main entrance)
- Infiltrate Tower Block D to reach Bungsu's cell

**Key Locations in Assault**:
1. **North Courtyard**: Initial landing zone, combat with guards
2. **Tower Block D Stairwell**: Fighting way up to Level 3
3. **Cell #17**: Emotional reunion with Bungsu
4. **Escape Route**: Attempt to fly off island

### Q017 — The Fall

**Rajawali Confrontation**:
- Rajawali intercepts escape attempt in Great Hall courtyard
- Boss fight: Sulung vs. Rajawali
- **Sulung loses** (Rajawali is far more powerful)
- **Bungsu is killed** in front of Sulung (tragedy)
- Sulung thrown from island, falls toward death

**Symbolic Fall**: 
- Physical fall from sky = fall from hope
- Everything Sulung fought for seemingly lost
- Darkest moment before ACT 3 transformation

### Q018-Q020 — Return & Vengeance

- Bungsu's soul becomes Jiwa Peri (spirit companion)
- Good Eagle provides Golden Wings (true flight ability)
- Sulung returns to Sky Island with new power
- Final battle in Great Hall
- Choice: Kill Rajawali OR expose truth publicly
